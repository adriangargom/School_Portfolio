Unreal Engine 5

Unreal Engine enables game developers and creators across industries to create next-generation 3D content and experiences in real-time with more freedom, fidelity, and flexibility than ever before.

After several years of development, Unreal Engine 5 was officially released on April 5, 2022. And can be downloaded by any user for free from the Epic Games store.

As we have already been able to verify, the engine has delighted us with some impressive technical demos showing the applications of the new technologies added to the Unreal Engine 5

New Mario movie

The Movie will transfer the Nintendo superstar and the essence of its games to a medium as different as the cinema, and it must be recognized that the project is in great hands: Shigeru Miyamoto, its creator, and it is being brought to life by the animation studio Illumination.
